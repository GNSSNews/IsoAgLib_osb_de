PCAN Driver
~~~~~~~~~~~

The driver is used in IsoAgLib at least for
  tools/can_server (make can_server_msq_pcan can_server_socket_pcan)
  bin/can_server_msq_pcan
  bin/can_server_socket_pcan
.

Please download the driver from

  http://p103112.typo3server.info/fileadmin/media/files/usb.zip

and place the content of 

  Disk/PCAN-Light

in this directory!
